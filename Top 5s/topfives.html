The 5 Places I Want To Visit Most

Italy

I want to visit Italy due to its beautiful architecture, amazing food, and interesting language and history. I also want to eat authentic Italian lasagna!

France

I really want to go to france to watch the PSG football team play in their hometown. My step-mother also lived in Paris for a few years and I want to relive the experience.

Japan

I went to Japan in June of 2023, and I really enjoyed it. I want to revisit Shinjuku, as well as Kyoto. I really enjoyed looking at the tori gates and beautiful shrines.

South Korea

I find the language of South Korea very pretty, as well as the scenery. I would like to visit Seoul.

Sweden

My father visited Stockholm a few years ago and loved it. I also would like to visit the different areas in Sweden, as it is a very beautiful country with a wide variety of different flora and fauna.
